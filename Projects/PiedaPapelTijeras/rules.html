Rock paper scissors Rules

Welcome, you are going to play rock paper and scissors game, the rules are simple, 2 players select one of the three options and count 1 2 3 rock paper scissors to make the movement at the same time, rock wins to scissor, scissor wins to paper and paper wins to rock. If players select the same option, then it is a tie, the Player who wins more matches in 10 attempts win the game. Enjoy!
